SHARE TRADERS TICKER TAPE

LIVE STREAMING OF STOCK MARKET DATA


A share trader, often known as a stock trader, is a person or an agent who trades stocks. Traders who trade on a daily basis are also known as day traders. Stock traders are many these days, as everyone involved in the financial sector has access to a wealth of knowledge on the stock market. Day traders and stock traders who acquire an affordable led stock ticker tape for their private offices or residences are Tickerplay's main customers and end consumers.


The most common sizes of ticker tapes purchased by traders are 5 feet, 8 feet, 20, 50, 100 and 500 feet etc. The most wellknown versions among stock traders are the economical and premium models. The market is gradually expanding, with an increasing number of stock traders exhibiting an interest in stock tickers, as led ticker tapes are an epitome of beauty that not only provides live stock and financial updates but also beautifies the surrounding environment. There are currently over 500 Tickerplay installations in North America for stock and day traders. To learn more about ticker models, go to the ticker models page. We can mount our ticker tapes on any given surface in your business or home.
