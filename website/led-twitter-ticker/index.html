LED TWITTER TICKER

LIVE STREAMING PERSONAL & PUBLIC TWEETS


Because of its brief communications, commonly known as "tweets," Twitter is one of the most popular social networking platforms. Tweets play an essential part in the business sector since they allow a message to go viral in a short amount of time. Most businesses prefer to display updates from a certain tweeter feed throughout the day to keep employees informed in real time. Our research and development team produced a software that can stream and scroll twitter feeds in response to the changing trend among ticker tape buyers to include twitter feeds in the segment.


From our entities, you can select your specific twitter ticker model. Throughout the day, you can receive updates from any individual Twitter feed, or you can rearrange between more than two tweets in a pre-determined sequence. You can show your customers the most recent tweets about your firm, as well as the most recent news. On your led twitter ticker tape, you may broadcast both real-time and delayed twitter feeds. Standard twitter ticker tape types are available in a variety of heights and resolutions, as well as viewing distances. Furthermore, if you require a custom solution, we may create one for your business, gallery, outdoor exhibition, schools or institutions, and so on.

We utilize a versatile module that can adjust to practically any curvature, allowing us to create seamless scrolling data such as stocks, RSS news, Cryptocurrencies, Commodities, and custom text, as well as gorgeous logos.
